What goes in compost?

  • Fruits, vegetables, meats
  • Coffee grounds, tea bags (remove any staples first)
  • Eggshells
  • Food-soiled paper/cardboard containers (wet paper coffee cups, pizza boxes, etc.)
  • Compostable packaging
  • Used napkins, tissues and paper towels

What doesn't go in compost?

  • Paper towels soaked with cleaning solution
  • Rocks and stones
  • Liquids

Moving Towards a Circular Economy

A circular economy is an economic model that aims to eliminate waste and the continual use of resources. It employs reuse, sharing, repair, refurbishment, remanufacturing and recycling to create a closed-loop system.
